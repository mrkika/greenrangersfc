About Green Rangers FC

Green Rangers FC is a professional football club based in Agbor, Delta State, Nigeria. Founded with the passion to empower local talent and bring pride to our community, the club has become a symbol of excellence, teamwork, and discipline.

With deep roots in the rich football culture of Delta State, Green Rangers FC is on a mission to develop future stars while thrilling fans with top-class performances on and off the field.
